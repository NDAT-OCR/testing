Alumni Spotlight

Mark Smith, founder and CEO of an education software company, has been passionate about entrepreneurship from a young age, founding his first company in high school.

After high school, Mark began looking for a college that would allow him to follow his passion for technology. When his father brought home a U.S. News and World Report featuring the Best Colleges in the U.S, Mark and his brother decided to take a road trip from Alabama – on the list was a tour of Sample University.

The rest, as they say, is history. Mark graduated from Sample University with a major in Computer Science. Like many students, Mark forged strong relationships with her professors that followed her beyond her college years. “The teachers at Sample University see who you are and encourage you in your interests. They saw that I had a mathematical, problem-solving mind and helped me figure out how I could tie that into the real world,” said Mark.
